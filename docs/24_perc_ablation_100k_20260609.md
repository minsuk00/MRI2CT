# U-Net perceptual-loss ablation (100k steps)

**Source HTML:** _html/perc_ablation_100k_20260609.html
**Date:** 2026-06-09
**TL;DR:** Single-axis contrast of perceptual loss on/off at the ep200/100k-step checkpoint (perceptual_w=0.1 vs 0.0, all else identical). The perceptual (NCC) variant slightly improves every metric: macro body MAE 33.0 vs 33.8 HU, Hard Bone Dice 0.810 vs 0.801, and the per-subject body-MAE improvement is significant (Wilcoxon p=6.2e-19).

Middle snapshot of the perc_ablation series (docs/23 dated 2026-06-03 at ep400/200k steps, docs/25 dated 2026-06-17 broadening to anatomix-injection site). Center-wise validation split, 207 subjects, all at the 100k-step (epoch 200) parity checkpoint, Track-A metrics + Hard Dice, identical definitions to full_eval_20260601.

## Variants

- no-perc (baseline) (9xmodnhn_ep200): perceptual_w=0.0, dice_w=0.1, n=207
- perceptual (ncc) (91hdk0ka_ep200): perceptual_w=0.1, dice_w=0.1, n=207

The two variants share identical config (dice_w=0.1, ssim_w=0.1, l1_w=1.0, center-wise split) and differ in the perceptual loss term only. They are nonetheless separate training runs (different seed/trajectory), so a small part of any gap is run-to-run noise.
